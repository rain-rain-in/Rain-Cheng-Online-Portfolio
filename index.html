About

Rain Cheng is a Hong Kong-based videographer and artist who crafts stylised imagery and sound to captivate and inspire audiences.

His practice is rooted in creative storytelling and the integration of diverse disciplines. Beyond traditional screen-based work, his interest in digital media installations drives him to explore new storytelling techniques and push the boundaries of narrative form.

He has worked with local creative agencies, which serve as platforms to channel his ideas into imaginative, visually driven narratives.

He holds a Bachelor of Social Science in Journalism and Communication (Creative Media) from The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK).
